About Me

Driven by purpose, discipline, and growth

Background

I am a pre-business student at Brigham Young University pursuing a future in business and entrepreneurship. I chose BYU not only for its strong academic reputation, but also for the unique spiritual environment and community that encourages both personal and professional growth. Being surrounded by motivated, values-driven individuals has strengthened my commitment to excellence and continuous improvement.

I am currently maintaining a 3.9 GPA while developing skills in business, technology, and problem-solving. My academic experience has provided a strong foundation for understanding how organizations operate and how innovative ideas can be turned into real-world solutions.

Professional Development

Through a variety of work experiences, I have developed a strong work ethic, discipline, and the ability to perform in fast-paced environments. From customer service roles to hands-on technical work, I have consistently demonstrated reliability, adaptability, and a commitment to improvement.

Additionally, my experience serving as a full-time missionary in Sonora, Mexico further strengthened my leadership and communication abilities. During this time, I became fluent in Spanish and gained valuable experience working with individuals from diverse backgrounds, helping me become more adaptable, resilient, and people-focused.

Goals & Aspirations

I am motivated by the opportunity to create, innovate, and build something meaningful. My long-term goal is to become a successful businessman and entrepreneur, with the ambition to start and grow my own business. I am especially drawn to opportunities that allow me to solve real problems and make a positive impact in the lives of others.

What drives me most is the ability to create my own path. I value the flexibility, responsibility, and challenge that come with entrepreneurship, and I am committed to developing the skills and mindset necessary to succeed in that environment.

Personal Strengths

I consider myself to be highly disciplined, hardworking, and goal-oriented. My background in athletics, including varsity basketball and swimming, has reinforced my ability to stay focused, compete, and perform under pressure. These experiences continue to shape how I approach both academic and professional challenges.

I am continually striving to grow, improve, and push myself beyond my comfort zone. I believe that consistent effort, strong values, and a willingness to learn are key to long-term success.
